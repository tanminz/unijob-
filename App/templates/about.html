Centralized Job Portal for VNU-HCM Students

UniJobs+ – Tailored Jobs for Every Major

UniJobs+ is the internal recruitment platform developed specifically for students across all VNU-HCM member universities. It syncs student academic data (GPA, major, credits...) to recommend suitable job and internship opportunities. Students can explore verified listings for internships, part-time, or full-time jobs posted by trusted employers.

Bridging Students, Employers, and Universities

Verified Employers – Transparent Hiring – Efficient Management.

Employers can publish job posts after undergoing a verification process. CTSV (Student Affairs Office) can approve listings, monitor applications, and generate reports by department or cohort. Companies can filter candidates based on major, GPA, and track the recruitment process efficiently and transparently.
